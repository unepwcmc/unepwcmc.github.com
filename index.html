Informatics.

"Using technology to make biodiversity easy"

UNEP-WCMC continued to lead the sector in coupling professional software engineering with science and policy expertise. In 2011-12 UNEP-WCMC built over 25 web based tools designed to manage, analyse and communicate environmental information. These tools helped nations report on the state of their environment faster than ever before, recruited the public to collect accurate information on protected areas and assisted policy makers in understanding how money is being spent to reduce emissions. This team consists of software engineers specialising in open source technology, product managers who understand the sector and ensure delivery of valuable tools, and geospatial engineers to continue UNEP-WCMC's long tradition of mapping biodiversity.

"The software we make is helping to save the planet - we take that seriously"

Our world class team stands out in the sector by being a highly skilled, efficient and professional group of software engineers, analysts, product managers and designers. 

We are lucky to work on some of the most interesting and important projects that have real world impacts. We collaborate with some of the best environmental organisations in the world to deliver data visualisation, decision support and information management tools.

Our work is built on open source software and the code we write is open. It’s one way we can share what we do with the world.
